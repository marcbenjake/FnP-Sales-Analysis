**FNP Sales Performance Dashboard**

**Overview**

This project features an interactive Business Intelligence dashboard designed to analyze and visualize sales performance for FNP (Ferns N Petals). By processing over 1,000 orders, this tool provides a comprehensive view of revenue drivers, seasonal trends, and logistical efficiency.

**Key Features**

- Executive KPIs: Real-time tracking of Total Revenue ($3.5M), Order Volume, and Average Order Value ($3,521).
- Temporal Analysis: Revenue breakdown by month and hour (order placed) to identify peak shopping windows (7 AM & 7 PM).
- Product & Category Insights: Deep dive into top-performing gift categories like "Colors" and "Soft Toys."
- Geographic Mapping: Identification of top 10 performing cities by order volume.
- Logistics Tracking: Monitoring of the 5.5-day average order-to-delivery cycle.

**Tools Used**

- Data Visualization: Excel
- Data Cleaning: Power Query Editor
- Analysis: Trend analysis, descriptive statistics, and logistical bottleneck identification.

**Business Insights Derived**

- Seasonality: Identified massive revenue spikes in February and August, suggesting a need for scaling marketing campaigns during these periods.
- Logistics: Highlighted a 5.5-day delivery average, identifying a key area for operational improvement to meet industry standards.
